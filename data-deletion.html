User Data Deletion Instructions

This page explains how to request the deletion of your personal data related to Cross Word The Game.

How to Request Data Deletion

  1. Send an email to crossblockthegame@outlook.com with the subject line “Data Deletion Request – Cross Word The Game”.
  2. Please include:
    • Your email address used in the game (or player ID if available).
    • Your platform (Android / iOS) and the date of the request.
  3. You may also use this form: Data Deletion Form (optional).

What Data Will Be Deleted?

All personal data stored for this game will be deleted, including: player profile, game progress, purchases linked to your player ID, advertising identifiers (if any), and related technical logs.

Processing Time and Confirmation

We process deletion requests within 30 days. Once your data has been deleted, you will receive an email confirmation. Please note that data deletion is irreversible (progress and in-app purchases cannot be restored).
